Overview

This is a simple website built for our ITEC 101 project! It acts as a bridge to link AUPP freshmen to our discord server.

The reason why we chose to make a discord server is based on the results of our survey. A discord server offers a lot of customisations for a better user experience. Aside from finding study partners, students could use this server for doing stuff like watching movies and gaming.

Preview

Students can introduce themselves here!

There are text channels for each faculty of undergraduate studies.

Students can use these study room voice channels to do assignments together.

Students can use lounge voice channels to do activities together.

We've enabled age-restriction in our server to prevent people under 18 from joining.
